Estate Agent Partnerships

Clear oil heating queries on rural and semi-rural property sales — fast, OFTEC-certified, surveyor-friendly.

Why Partner With Us?

Rural and semi-rural property sales routinely stall on oil heating queries: a missing service history on the boiler, a tank that does not meet BS 5410 fire-separation distances, an old single-skin tank, an undersized bund or a damaged supply line. By partnering with OilCert, your agency can clear these queries quickly with a single phone call and keep the sale moving.

What You Get

  • Priority Booking — Your clients get first availability. Most services or tank inspections are booked within 48 hours of referral.
  • 24-Hour CD/10 Turnaround — Digital CD/10 commissioning & servicing certificates and tank condition reports delivered the next working day.
  • Dedicated Account Manager — A single point of contact for your agency. No call centres, no runaround.
  • Competitive Referral Pricing — Volume-based pricing for agencies referring multiple oil-heated properties per month.
  • Surveyor-Ready Reports — Tank inspection reports cover age, construction, bund integrity, BS 5410 separation distances and supply pipework — exactly what RICS surveyors flag.
  • Compliance Support — We help your team understand OFTEC TI/133, OFTEC TI/134 and BS 5410 so you can advise clients confidently when an offer is on the table.

How It Works

Getting started is simple:

  • Step 1: Register your agency by completing the contact form or calling our partnerships team.
  • Step 2: Receive your agency referral process and pricing.
  • Step 3: Refer clients directly. We handle booking, the service or tank inspection, and the certificate.
  • Step 4: The CD/10 or tank condition report is emailed to you and your client within 24 hours.

Common Scenarios We Help With

  • Pre-sale annual services to evidence a current service history on the boiler
  • Tank condition reports when a surveyor flags an old or single-skin tank
  • Pre-purchase oil heating inspections requested by buyers or solicitors
  • Landlord oil safety records for managed rural lettings portfolios
  • Remedial work pricing on the boiler, flue, tank or supply line before the property is listed
